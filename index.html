    A meetings app for ownCloud.

    ownMeeting allows ownCloud users to negotiate meeting times with other ownCloud users, as well as outside attendees. Unlike similar options, ownCloud does not mine the attendees information to create profiles.

    Attendees are prompted to vote for proposed time blocks that work with their schedule. Attendees are send the voting page link via email.

    Based on the attendee voting results the organizer can make the final decision on meeting time.

    Supported Browsers

    • Latest Firefox
    • Latest Chrome/Chromium
    • Latest Safari

    We do not support IE or Edge, though patches are accepted.
